\section{Alignment}
\textit{\TeX\ \say{glues} horizontal and then vertical \say{boxes} together to make lines, paragraphs / pages (respectively). \TeX\ \smash{primitives\textsuperscript{\textdagger}} (\textbf{cmds} \& \textit{params}) afford fine-grained control of placement. For narrow eqn, force box height / depth to 0pt with }\texttt{\textbackslash smash}.
